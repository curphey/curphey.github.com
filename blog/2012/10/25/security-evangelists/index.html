Who Pays That Person?

Oct 25th, 2012

The security industry seems to be brimming with security evangelists. These are not to be confused with a Televangelist: or should they? Every time I cross-paths with an evangelist of any kind I ask myself a simple question: who pays them?

It is difficult to get a man to understand something when his job depends on not understanding it.
