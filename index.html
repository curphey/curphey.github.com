About Me

I grew up in the UK where I earned a Masters Degree in Information Security specializing in cryptography. After working at investment banks in the City of London, I moved to the States in 2000 to join Internet Security Systems (now part of IBM). I ran software security at Charles Schwab and founded OWASP, joined Foundstone as an early employee, a company then sold to McAfee. After the aquisition I reported to the President for four years working across the company running services and research and development teams and supporting corporate mergers and aquisitions. After a sabatical in France I ran the security tools team at Microsoft and the MSDN Subscriptions business before founding SRC:CLR (pronounced SourceClear) in early 2014.

SRC:CLR - security for open-source code.
